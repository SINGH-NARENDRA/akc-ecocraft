About AKC EcoCraft

AKC EcoCraft, by Aman Kumar and Co., is a Kolkata-based manufacturer and exporter of eco-friendly jute and cotton bags. We help businesses move toward sustainable packaging with products that combine durability, quality, and customization.

Whether you need bags for retail, gifting, promotions, packaging, or export orders, we provide dependable production support for both standard and customized requirements.

Business-focused manufacturing: built for wholesale, gifting, retail and export requirements.
Flexible customization: available across sizes, prints, styles and branding needs.
Quality-first production: designed for repeat use and dependable supply.
